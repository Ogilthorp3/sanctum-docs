<div class="sanctum-brand-statement">

The name means *sacred inner chamber*. A dark square holds an amber diamond at its center: the thing you care about most, inside the thing strong enough to protect it.

In a year when every major platform is racing to ingest personal data for model training, the idea of a private inner chamber has gone from metaphor to straight up survival strategy.
