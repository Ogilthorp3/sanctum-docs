## Cloud AI Budget

Paid tiers include a monthly cloud AI budget for requests that go beyond your local models — complex reasoning, code generation, long-context analysis. The kind of thinking that makes your Mac Mini's fan spin up and say "not this one, chief." Usage beyond the included budget is billed at cost + 20%.

All cloud requests pass through the PII anonymization layer. Personal data (names, emails, phone numbers) is scrubbed before it leaves your network and restored when the response returns. Your external AI provider never sees who you are. You are a ghost who asks very specific questions about YAML configuration.

## FAQ

**Can I use Sanctum without paying anything?**
Yes. Core runs entirely on your hardware with local models. No cloud, no subscription, no limits. No catch. We are aware this sounds suspicious in 2026. We understand your skepticism. It is warranted by the behavior of every other company.

**What happens if I cancel a paid plan?**
You keep Core. Your config, automations, and local agents continue working. Cloud features and multi-site sync stop. Your house does not forget how to be a house.

**Is there a free trial for paid tiers?**
Every paid tier has a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. We are not going to auto-charge you on day 15 and hope you do not notice. That business model is for people who hate their customers.

**Do you sell my data?**
No. We never have your data. Everything runs on your hardware. That is the entire point. If we wanted your data, we would not have built a platform specifically designed to keep it away from us.
